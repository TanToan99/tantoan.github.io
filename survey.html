FRIEND WITH BENEFITS OR NOT ?
REPORT ABOUT SURVEY

Introduction

In recent years in Vietnam, love relationships have become deadlocked because of the covid epidemic in the past 2 years, causing many couples to break up because they cannot solve physiological problems. Therefore, the Friend with benefits (FWB) relationship is of great interest to many people. So we created the survey as a study designed to learn more about the communication that occurs in the FWB relationship with Benefits.

The purpose of this study?

This form of interpersonal relationship is becoming increasingly popular among people in their early to mid-20’s but has not yet been thoroughly researched. Thus, this study will begin to look at the communication that occurs between partners engaging in Friends with Benefits relationships. For this study, Friends with Benefits Relationships are defined as: A sexual relationship with a friend whom you are not dating at the time of sexual involvement and do not have any intention of dating. The relationship lacks commitment, a desire for a romantic relationship, and emotional.

Analyze

Through the survey that we have created, here are some actual data of the survey participants:
  • The level of interest in the relationship of friend with benefits in our area is mostly from the age group of 20-22: 40% of participants are in their 20s, 20% of participants are in their 22s, and 40% are again at the age of 21.
  • People with the age of friendship with a beneficial relationship between 18 and 20 years old: 40% of the participants made friends for a benefit in their 20s, 20% at the age of 19, and 40% at the age of 18.
  • Gender related to FWB relationship: 40% is Male, 60% is Female.
  • FWB relationship types that participants are referencing: 40% Perious FWB, 60% Current FWB.
  • Length of time thier FWB partner was friend to becoming sexually involved: 40% from 1-10 days, 40% from 11-30 days, 20% from 1 month or more.
  • The length of time the FWB relationship exists between them and their partner: about 60% from 1 month back, 20% from 1-3 months, 20% from 4-6 months.
  • Participants will decide who is allowed to know about their relationship: 80% will actually agree and 20% will strongly agree.
  • Participants feel high with their partner in FWB relationship: 20% of participants slightly agree, 40% of participants actually agree and 40% of participants strongly agree.
  • Participants expected their partners to cooperate in sexual intercourse: 20% of participants were neutral, 60% of participants actually agreed, and 20% of participants strongly agreed.
  • Participants thought that they were not the ones who would put an end to this relationship first: 20% strongly disagree, 40% disagree, 30% neutral, and 20% strongly agree.
  • hen the relationship ended, the participants regretted or hurt because of the FWB relationship: 60% Agree and 40% strongly agree

Suggestion

The issue of whether to start a FWB relationship or not depends on each person's views and thoughts. In the past, many people believed that this relationship went against morality, especially those who were married, so they were strongly opposed.
However, in today's society, in Western countries, this issue has become much more open. Therefore, this out-of-the-way relationship is still welcomed by many people.
Some other young people think that starting a FWB relationship is not advisable. Because this relationship will not last forever, if it lasts more or less, it will also hurt you. Especially, for those who have families, what if the other side knows the truth?
So, before deciding whether to start a relationship with "no strings attached", please carefully consider the risks that you may have to bear after ending it!
Because an FWB relationship needs the following elements:
- Both are mature, financially independent, thinking mature and able to make their own decisions about their own lives.
- They can care for each other but absolutely must not ask for anything from the other in terms of love, future, marriage.
- Willing to share, experience anything about sex in a fun way without feeling uncomfortable.
- Ending the relationship FWB still shows respect for each other.
Hopefully the survey will help you get a satisfactory answer to the question, what is FWB? Whether to start a “friends with benefits” relationship or not. If you do the above and are willing to accept it then a FWB relationship can begin. Because should or not depends on the personal opinion of each person.
